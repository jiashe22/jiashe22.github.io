Attraction Kaohsiung Cultural Center A center located in Lingya District of Kaohsiung, Taiwan. It was founded by the city government in 1981. The main building of the center is a complex of two concert halls, many galleries and a library. The cultural center built as a memorial hall for Chiang Kai-shek. The cultural center is accessible within by Kaohsiung MRT Museum of Fine Arts The museum of fine art is an art gallery and it is located in the Gushan District. They have 4 floors in the museum, they each have a lot of rooms for exhibition. The Museum is open from Tuesday to Sunday, 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m., and is closed on Mondays. National Science and Technology Museum (NSTM) National Science and Technology Museum, is a museum about science and technology. Sometimes there will be exhibition in the museum which is about science, sometimes art. There are 17 part in the museum, each having different topic.
